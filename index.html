About Me

I'm an undergraduate at Physics Department of Wuhan University and also a student in Hongyi Honor College, which is called "Mount Everest Plan" in our country. Now, I'm working in our School's Astrophysics Center. I have wide interests on astronomy research especially about stellar astrophysics, high-energy astrophysics and cosmology. I am currently doing a project about the water maser and sometime I'm also analysing the data from HXMT(The Hard X-ray Modulation Telescope) in order to do another project about the spin of the black hole.
